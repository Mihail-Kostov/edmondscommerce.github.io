Prevent Ctrl+S Freezing Your Terminal

As someone who types a lot of text everyday and am generally quite keen not to lose my work, I tend to hit the key combination [Ctrl]+[S] quite a lot. In fact its almost become muscle memory that every sentence or two I will hit that combination.

When working on a terminal, this can have the unfortunate outcome that the terminal freezes up. For more time than I would like to admit, I thought that this was a Linux bug And found myself frequently restarting terminal sessions that had locked up.
