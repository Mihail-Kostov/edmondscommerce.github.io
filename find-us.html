Directions to Edmonds Commerce

Edmonds Commerce are based in Shipley, West Yorkshire in England, United Kingdom.

Telephone: 01274 590036

Postal Address: Crowgill Chambers Crowgill Road Shipley West Yorkshire BD18 3SN

General Directions

Shipley is well connected with rail, road and air connections all in the vicinity.

The office entrance is located on Crowgill Road which is a side street coming off Saltaire Road. The turn off is across the road from “Bubbles” Hand car wash. Directly on the other side of Crowgill Road from us is a large Motorbike dealership specialising in Ducati and Triumph.

Directions by Rail

Shipley has a well connected train station. You can see the Shipley Live departure board for more details.

If you are travelling from another area by rail, you are most likely to go to Leeds and then connect from Leeds to Shipley on the Skipton train or the Bradford train. It is the first stop out of Leeds on either of these trains – around 10 minutes journey time.

Directions by Road

If you are driving to us, the best thing to do is aim for the parking rather than our office directly.

There are two places you can park. For short visits (2 hours stay) you can park without charge on Crowgill Road.

If you are expecting to be longer than 2 hours then there is a pay and display carpark also on Crowgill Road.

Directions by Air

Leeds Bradford Airport Our nearest airport is Leeds Bradford Airport which is well connected across the UK and Europe with some trans atlantic connections also.

Manchester Airport A much bigger airport that is only 1 hour away on the road is Manchester Airport which is very well connected internationally.
